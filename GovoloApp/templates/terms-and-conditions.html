2. Booking Policies

All bookings are subject to availability. A booking is only confirmed once we have received the required deposit or full payment and issued a confirmation receipt.

  • A 30% deposit is required at the time of booking for most tours.
  • Full payment must be made at least 15 days prior to the tour start date.

3. Cancellation & Refund Policy

We understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • Cancellations made 30+ days before the tour: Full refund (minus admin fees).
