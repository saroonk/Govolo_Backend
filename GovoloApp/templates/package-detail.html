Detailed Itinerary

DAY 01
Arrival in Hanoi & Heritage Check-in
  • Arrive at Noi Bai International Airport where your private chauffeur awaits.
  • Transfer to your luxury boutique hotel in the heart of Hanoi's Old Quarter.
  • Enjoy a welcome dinner featuring traditional Northern Vietnamese cuisine.
DAY 02
Hanoi City Soul & Culinary Walk

Explore the Temple of Literature and Ho Chi Minh Complex. In the afternoon, embark on a curated food tour through hidden alleys to discover the best Bún Chả and Egg Coffee in the city.

DAY 03
Halong Bay Luxury Cruise

Travel to Halong Bay and board a 5-star luxury cruise. Spend the day kayaking through limestone karsts, visiting hidden caves, and enjoying a sunset gala dinner on the deck.

DAY 04
Morning Tai Chi & Flight to Central Vietnam

Start the day with Tai Chi on the deck. After brunch, transfer to the airport for your flight to Da Nang. Upon arrival, a short drive brings you to the magical town of Hoi An.

DAY 05
Hoi An Ancient Town & Rice Fields

Discover the Japanese Covered Bridge and heritage houses. Later, take a peaceful bike ride through verdant rice paddies and enjoy a basket boat ride in the coconut forest.

DAY 06
Leisure & Departure

Enjoy a final breakfast overlooking the river. Have some free time for last-minute tailor-made clothing before your private transfer to Da Nang airport for departure.
